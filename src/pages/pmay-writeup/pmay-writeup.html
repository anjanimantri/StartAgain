PMAY Write-up

    Good news for all first time home buyers as they can save upto 2.67 Lakhs through home loan subsidy which is given by government of India. Refer below mentioned table for details.

    Hon’ble Prime Minister envisioned Housing for all by 2022 when the Nation completes 75 years of its Independence. In order to achieve this objective, Central Government has launched a comprehensive mission ‘’ Housing for All by 2022’’. Also popularly known as ‘’PRADHAN MANTRI AWAS YOJNA or CREDIT LINK SUBSIDY SCHEME’’

How to get the Home Loan Subsidy?

1. Apply for home loan with a Bank/Financial institution.
2. If eligible for subsidy, your application will be forwarded to the Central Nodal Agency (CNA).
3. If it's approved, the CNA will disburse the subsidy amount to the Banker/Financial instition.
4. This will be credited to your Loan account, thus reducing the total loan amount.
5. For instance, if your annual income is Rs 6 lakh and the loan amount is Rs 9 lakh, then subsidy will be upto 2.67 Lakhs.
6. When this is deducted, the loan is reduced to 6.33 Lakhs. You will pay EMI on this lowered amount.

For more information you can refer detailed guidelines issued by the government of India @
